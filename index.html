About Us

We are manufacturer specialized in neoprene producing, including fishing safety, hunting safety, diving sports safety and so on. Export to USA, Japan and European countries. Our products contains fishing, hunting wader ( water proof and breath freely, neoprene, nylon PVC 100% water proof ) gloves ( water proof gloves ) accessories and sports gloves safety. From 2000 our factory founded till now, we have buildings 5000 square meters with 100 skillful workers. We are skilled in producing waders and so on. From chose neoprene ( normal and enviromental ) and various of cloth, to finish the shipment, we have professional team to work for it. Welcome to contact us and visit our factory.

About Us
