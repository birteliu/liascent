About Us

We are manufacturer specialized in neoprene producing, including fishing safety, hunting safety, diving sports safety and so on. Export to USA, Japan and European countries. Our products contains fishing, hunting wader (water proof and breath freely, neoprene, nylon PVC 100% water proof ) gloves ( water proof gloves ) accessories and sports gloves safety.

From 2000 our factory founded till now, we have buildings 5000 square meters with 100 skillful workers. We are skilled in producing waders and so on. From chose neoprene ( normal and enviromental ) and various of cloth, to finish the shipment, we have professional team to work for it.

Welcome to contact us and visit our factory.

關於我們

我們是一家專業從事 Neoprene 的生產廠，主要生產包括釣魚、打獵、潛水、運動護具等產品，出口到美日歐等國家。主要產品有釣魚打獵 wader ( 防水透氣、neoprene、nylonpvc 三種百分百防水 )、釣魚打獵潛水手套 ( 防水手套 )、配件及運動手套護具。

從 2000 年設廠至今目前工廠廠房大約 5000 平方公尺，將近百人員工，專業從事釣魚褲等站品生產。從原物料 neoprene ( 一般、環保兩種 ) 和各種布料的種類，到產品的完成出貨，都是專業的團隊生產線。
