About Us

We are the profession manufacturer for Neoprene products. Factory was established in year 2000 and for last 20 years, we are focus for overseas market included for USA, Europe, and Japan etc.

We specify in Wader for Fishing & Hunting, material included for Neoprene, different kind of waterproof, breath freely, Nylon PVC 100% water proof. And all kind of glove for fishing, diving, hunting, and related accessories.

According to the experience for last 20 years, we have professional team from material choose to complete the product to fulfill for customer requirement. Please do not hesitate to contact with us if you need anything.

關於我們

我們是專業製造 Neoprene 產品的工廠，從西元 2000 開廠至今，已經接近 20 年的生產經驗，主要是外銷市場為主。

目前客戶涵蓋美國、日本和歐洲等，主要的產品是釣魚打獵褲，材質涵蓋 Neoprene 及多種防水透氣材質，另外也有釣魚打獵相關配件如釣魚、濳水防水手套和由 Neoprene 材質製作的運動保護護具。

將近二十年來的專業經驗，我們由材質的選擇至生產完成都有專業的人員負責、處理及達成客戶的需求，目前工廠的面積是 5000 平方公尺，並有一百人員左右。
